Tank to Tankless: Technical Requirements

Gas & Venting Infrastructure

  • Requires a dedicated gas line capable of delivering 199,000 BTU/h (usually 3/4" or larger).
  • New Category III/IV stainless steel or Schedule 40 PVC venting directly through the roof or exterior wall.
  • Standard 120V electrical outlet for the unit's digital panel and electronic ignition.

Code Compliance

  • Dedicated isolation valves for annual flushing and maintenance.
  • Condensate neutralizer kit (if discharging into cast iron or public sewer).
